How to Brew a Perfect Cup of Coffee

Brewing a perfect cup of coffee starts with high-quality beans, freshly ground. Measure the coffee-to-water ratio; a common ratio is one to two tablespoons of coffee per six ounces of water. Let the coffee steep for about four minutes before pressing or pouring it out. Enjoy your coffee black or with your preferred additions.
